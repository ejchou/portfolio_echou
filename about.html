Steward. Dreamer. Systems Thinker.

Forever curious and fueled by fresh air, I've lived in many cities with a winding path that's taken me through music, finance, food, and now digital design. While my path hasn’t been linear, one thing has remained constant: my obsession with understanding how people think, feel, and move through the world.

I love a good puzzle, especially the kind you solve with others. I’m a big fan of asking “what if?” and “why not?” even if it means playing devil’s advocate. I believe the best experiences are born when we color outside the lines.

I’m inspired by the natural world and fascinated by how things grow and adapt, whether it’s ideas, ecosystems, or communities. When I’m not designing, you can find me behind a camera, traveling to a far-off land, or heading for the mountains to climb, hike, or ski.
