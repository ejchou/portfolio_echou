About Me

My Story

Inspired by nature, a curious learner, and an avid wanderlust, I constantly find myself beyond constrained boundaries. My whole life I’ve never lived anywhere for very long and while I don’t label any one place as home, each has shaped me into the person I am today.

Unsurprisingly, my professional background is almost as varied as the places I’ve lived -- from music to finance to food to design. However, the one thing that has always stood true is my passion for understanding the complexities of human behavior and how we coexist with the world around us.

I thrive in collaborative environments and thoroughly enjoy troubleshooting. I’m a perfectionist at heart and am always eager to provide the best experience. I’m a firm believer in playing the role of devil’s advocate because nothing in this world is strictly right or wrong. Designing outside of the box (again, I love going out of bounds) is the difference between a solid experience to an outstanding experience.

Aiding my designs are my personal interests in photography, agroecology, and anthropology. When I’m not working, you can find me climbing, hiking, or skiing in the mountains.
